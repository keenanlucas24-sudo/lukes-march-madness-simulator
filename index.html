Methodology

Team Ratings

Each team is assigned a composite power rating derived from KenPom adjusted efficiency margin, NET rankings, and win-loss record strength. Ratings are normalized to produce win probabilities for each matchup.

Win Probability

Game outcomes use a logistic model: P(A wins) = 1 / (1 + 10^((R_B - R_A) / 15)), where R is the team's power rating. This produces realistic upset rates aligned with historical seed-matchup data.

Monte Carlo Engine

The full 63-game tournament is simulated thousands of times. Each simulation randomly resolves every game using the win probabilities. Results are aggregated to show how often each team reaches each round.
