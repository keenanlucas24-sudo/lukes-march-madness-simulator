### Non-Conference Record & Conference Impressiveness

**The Big 12's .801 NC record** is the most impressive in the country — their teams beat non-conference opponents at an 80% clip, validating their in-conference battles are real preparation for March. Arizona (32-2 on the season), Houston, and Iowa State all have résumés validated by tough non-conference scheduling.

**The Big Ten's .792 NC record** reflects a conference where even bubble teams (Iowa, Ohio State) beat quality out-of-conference opponents. Michigan's 31-3 record includes wins over multiple top-25 teams.

**The SEC's .759 NC record**, while strong, is slightly weaker than the Big 12/Big Ten, suggesting the 10-bid haul partially reflects a cannibalistic conference where SEC teams beat each other but not always the outside world. That said, Florida, Arkansas, and Vanderbilt all have credible at-large résumés.
